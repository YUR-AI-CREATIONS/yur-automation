## If They're Skeptical

**"Will it really work?"**

Yes. Download, run RUN.bat, it works. If your Python is broken or something is weird, use Docker - it works on any machine.

**"How long does it take?"**

30 seconds on the first run. 5 seconds every time after that.

**"What if I don't understand it?"**

Look at the dashboard. It's self-explanatory. Beautiful interface with your business numbers. If you want to dig deeper, read the docs.

**"Is my data safe?"**

Everything runs locally on your computer. Nothing leaves your system. No cloud, no third-party APIs. Fully air-gapped.

**"What if I break it?"**

Just delete the `data/` folder and run again. Starts fresh.

**"This sounds too good to be true."**

It's real. Try it for 5 minutes. If you don't like it, delete the folder.
