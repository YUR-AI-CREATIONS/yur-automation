## WHAT CANNOT BE AUTOMATED (Humans Still Needed)

### **1. Closing Large Deals** (>$50K)
- Needs human credibility and judgment
- Negotiation requires flexibility
- Build relationship/trust
- **AI can prep you, but you sign**

### **2. On-site Meetings/Demos**
- Some customers want to meet you
- Travel required
- Personal connection matters
- **AI can handle all prep + follow-up**

### **3. Contract Negotiation**
- Legal terms need human review
- Some customers modify terms
- Judgment calls ("do we accept XX?")
- **AI can review, flag, suggest, but you decide**

### **4. Executive Relationships**
- CEO/CFO may only talk to you
- Strategic partnership decisions
- Trust-based
- **AI can arrange and prepare, but you handle**
